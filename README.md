## About Govardhanapuram Gosala

Govardhanapuram Gosala is a sanctuary dedicated to providing shelter, care, and protection to cows. A gosala (also spelled goshala) is a traditional Hindu institution where cows are cared for and protected, especially those that are abandoned, injured, or elderly.
