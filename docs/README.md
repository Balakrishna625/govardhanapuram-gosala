## About Gosala

A gosala (गोशाला in Sanskrit) is a sanctuary for cows, especially those that are:
- Abandoned or orphaned
- Injured or disabled
- Elderly and no longer productive
- Rescued from slaughter

### Importance of Gosala

Gosalas play a vital role in:
- **Animal Welfare**: Providing care and protection to cows in need
- **Cultural Preservation**: Maintaining traditional Hindu values of cow protection
- **Environmental Benefits**: Supporting sustainable farming practices
- **Community Service**: Providing valuable resources like organic fertilizer and dairy products
